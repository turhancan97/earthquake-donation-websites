Helping the Victims of the Turkey/Syria Earthquakes
As you already may know, February 6th two earthquakes magnitudes 7.7 and 7.5 have hit an affected 10 cities in Turkey.
The earthquakes in Turkey and Syria have had devastating consequences, and the country is in need of support and assistance. Over 10,000 buildings have been destroyed, and 50,000 people are currently trapped under the rubble. The earthquakes have affected over 13 million people, and the actual death toll is estimated to be as high as 20,000. The situation is dire, and the country has declared a three-month state of emergency to respond to the tragedy.
